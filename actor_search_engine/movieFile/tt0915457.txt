 Falco - Verdammt, wir leben noch! (2008)Summaries The life story of 80s Austrian superstar Falco, the man behind the monster hit "Rock me, Amadeus". The story of Johann "Falco" Hlzel from young kid, the road from a band to his solo career, and to his tragic death.
