 The House of Branching Love (2009)Summaries A sort of "Divorce Finnish Style," Mika Kaurismki's rambunctious comedy, The House of Branching Love , recounts the breakup of a thirty-something professional couple - Juhani, a family therapist, and his wife, Tuula, a successful business trainer.
