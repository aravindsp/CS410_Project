 Urban Rush (20012013)Summaries Urban Rush (The Rush) was an hour-long talk show focused on the Vancouver entertainment industry. Forbes and co-hosts had over 30,000 interviews with guests involved in movies, music, TV, literature, theatre and culture. Produced by Shaw TV, it aired for 18 years.
