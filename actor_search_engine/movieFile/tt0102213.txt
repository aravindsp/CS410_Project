 Killing Streets (1991)Summaries When his twin brother is reported killed in a Beirut car explosion, a man turns the war-torn city upside-down in search of the truth. A young Marine is kidnapped by terrorists in Lebanon. His twin brother, frustrated at his inability to get any kind of information from the government about his brother's plight, travels to Lebanon with a plan for his own rescue operation.
