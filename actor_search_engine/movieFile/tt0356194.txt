 The Empty Pillow (1957)Summaries The story of first teenager love and its fake effect in a man's life. Salah is a young man living the best days of his life with his first love Samiha. Soon, Salah's dreams deteriorate when Samiha accepts a proposal from a wealthy man, and he eventually tries to overcome and start a new life with another woman.
