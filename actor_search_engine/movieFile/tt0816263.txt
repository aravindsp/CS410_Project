 Truth Be Told (2006)Summaries Actor Kelly Lopez seeks the truth from Hollywood Casting Directors by having a spell cast on them. Be careful what you ask for. Actor Kelly Lopez simply wants to be told the truth when auditioning in Hollywood. She has a psychic cast a spell on Hollywood insiders so that they must unwittingly tell her the truth about what they think of her. These "truths" have Kelly begging to be lied to again.
