 Hide and Seek (1997)Summaries Eight high school seniors, long-time friends, sneak into a large photo studio for an all-night party prior to graduation. They plan to play games they played when they were kids. Innocent kissing games played in seventh grade take on new meaning on the verge of adulthood. Startling revelations emerge during a game of truth. Loyalties change. Relationships realign. All the night's emotion and passion erupts in the stunning final sequence, a game of "Hide and Seek" like you've never seen before.
