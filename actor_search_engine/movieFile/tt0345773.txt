 Pasan ko ang daigdig (1987)Summaries Lupe, a poor woman, wishes to terminate her long-suffering and poverty, and improve not only her own life but that of her crippled mother, through her singing. With the help of her three friends (Luming, Tony, and Carding). she is determined to defeat and overcome the ills, traps, and harsh realities of life.
