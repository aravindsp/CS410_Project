 Sommerferien (1998)Summaries The Blue Fairy is the leader of the young fairies. Together with her companions, she is going to find the lost treasure. She is supported by the old wise fairies. On their travels they discover many magic secrets and get to know many different fairy clans. The various clans are specialized in various disciplines.
