 Beyond the Wave (2017)Summaries Complex relationship between one man and two women in an internal post apocalyptic landscape Starring Casper Van Dien (Starship Troopers, Sleepy Hollow), and produced by luxury pleasure brand LELO, Beyond the Wave follows one man's journey in a post-apocalyptic world where men and women choose to live apart. A chance meeting sparks an unforgettable journey, one that shows him the importance of understanding another's perspective. On the surface it's a love story, but deeper than that, it's a reminder on how to enrich relationships in an increasingly individualistic and divided world.
