 Love and a .45 (1994)Summaries A small time crook flees to Mexico to evade the authorities, loan sharks, and his murderous ex-partner with only his fianc and a trusted Colt .45. Watty Watts (Gil Bellows) has made a living out of robbing convenience stores, but after one of these jobs turned into murder by his partner, the psychopath Billy Mack Black (Rory Cochrane), he is on the run with his fianc Starlene Cheatham (Rene Zellweger) and with both Billy Mack, the Police, and some loan sharks on his trail.
