 The Sender (1998)Summaries An Air Force captain pursues the government agents who kidnapped his daughter after discovering that she has incredible "sending" powers. In 1965, an American fighter squadron encountered a spaceship while flying over the Bermuda Triangle. After getting into a brief dogfight with it, one of the fighters is shot down, and the others agree to keep what they've seen a secret. Years later, the downed aircraft is recovered, and the son of the missing pilot insists on seeing it. Soon, strange ocurrences start happening around the son and his family.
