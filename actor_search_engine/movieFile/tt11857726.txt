 Master Minds (2020 )Summaries Three celebrity contestants, Ken Jennings, Muffy Marracco, and Johnathan Corbblah face off against mere mortals in "Masterminds," hosted by Brooke Burns. Game Show Network's "Master Minds" debuted on April 6, 2020, hosted by Brooke Burns and regularly featuring Ken Jennings, Muffy Marracco, Jonathan Corbblah, Arianna Haut, and Ryan Chaffee. Previously, a slightly different version this show had been titled "Best Ever Trivia Show" and was hosted by Sherri Shepherd, with a debut date of June 10, 2019, featuring many of the same "Experts" which are now called "Master Minds."
