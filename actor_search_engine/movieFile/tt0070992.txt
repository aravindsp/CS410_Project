 Happy Days (19741984)Summaries The Cunningham family live through the 1950s with help and guidance from the lovable and almost superhuman greaser, Fonzie. Richie Cunningham and his friend Potsie face life at Jefferson High in Milwaukee Wisconsin in the 1950s. Lots of changes over time as kids come and go, new series spin off, Richie and pals go to college then the army. Even marriage.
