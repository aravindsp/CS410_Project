 Doctor Who (2005 )44) The further adventures in time and space of the alien adventurer known as the Doctor and their companions from planet Earth. The Doctor, a Time Lord from the race whose home planet is Gallifrey, travels through time and space in their ship the TARDIS (an acronym for Time and Relative Dimension In Space) with numerous companions. From time to time, the Doctor regenerates into a new form.
