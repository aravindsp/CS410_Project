 Alexander IRL (2017)Summaries Alex and EJ are brothers with nothing in common. Alex is a straight A student who desperately wants to be cool and win the affection of Lo (the coolest girl in school). EJ is a former high school legend living at home, wasting away in middle management in GE's innovation lab with dreams of becoming the next Mark Zuckerberg. When Alex and EJ's parents leave town for their second honeymoon, the boys join forces to throw a legendary high school party with hopes of making Alex the king of his class and EJ the next great app developer.
