 Tweedledum Torpedoed by Cupid (1916)Summaries Tweedledum, a farm hand, elopes with the daughter of his employer. He is pursued by a German Marine inventor and an English Lord, who are both favored suitors for the girl's hand. Tweedledum and Tweedledee embark on board a steamer for their honeymoon, when the Marine inventor launches a torpedo which blows up the steamer.
