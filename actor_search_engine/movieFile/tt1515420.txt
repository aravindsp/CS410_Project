 I Kissed a Vampire (2009 )Summaries A musical look at the torment of falling in love when you're in the middle of becoming a vampire. Dylan is terrified when a nasty bat bite transforms him into one of the living dead. His world is turned upside down, and he has to figure out how to balance his budding romance with Sara, the girl next door, and the bloodthirsty desires his magnetic vampire mentor Trey is constantly urging him to give in to.
