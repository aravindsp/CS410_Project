 Drakensang: The Dark Eye (2008 Video Game)Summaries Drakensang is a third-person party-based RPG based on the pen and paper role-play rules of The Dark Eye. Drakensang is the first PC game for over 10 years to be based on Germany's most successful and popular role-play system. Drakensang builds on the pen and paper rules as applied in version 4.0.
