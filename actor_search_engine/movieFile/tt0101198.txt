Rita left the show to care for her and Chris's unborn child. <silkstalkings@usa.net> Rita Lee Lance and Chris Lorenzo (each with the nickname of "Sam") solve high-profile crimes of passion for the Palm Beach Police Department. They'll stop at nothing in their pursuit of those who, due to their power or fame, feel that they are above the law of the land.
