 Cool as Ice (1991)Summaries A rapper gets stuck in a small town and falls for a local girl whose family is in witness protection. The old teen rebel saga is updated for the rap crowd, unfortunately rapper Vanilla Ice is the teen. Ice shows up on a neon-yellow motorcycle which gets everyone's attention, including the female honor student who has never had a rebellious bone in her body.
