 Manny & Lo (1996)Summaries A pregnant teen and her younger sister run away from foster homes and kidnap a woman whom they believe can help with the pregnancy. Two sisters, Amanda, 11, and Laurel, 16, run away from their different foster parents, sleeping in model homes, or wherever they can. But when Laurel becomes pregnant, they find they can't make it through this crisis on their own. With nowhere else to turn, they decide to kidnap Elaine, a clerk in a baby supply store.
