 Libertad para la juventud (1970)Summaries Three young sisters from a respectable wealthy family decide to revolt against their father's authority and his strict morality. In their quest for happiness and freedom the sisters turn to a life style of careless fun and debauchery. They soon find out that their behavior jeopardizes their relationships with boyfriends and might even endanger their own lives. Is freedom worth the risks?
