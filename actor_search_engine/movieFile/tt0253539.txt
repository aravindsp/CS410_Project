 Fed Up (1973)Summaries Bert, a young Belgian advertiser, is forced to carry out his military service, a long and painful journey for him. He strongly feels the absence of Jonquille, his fiance. On the other hand, he clashes with everybody else, whether his superiors like Captain Luxembourg and Commander Stella or the other officer cadets. He has only one friend, Michel, a communist...
