 Casualty (1986 )Summaries The everyday lives of the people frequenting the frenetic Accident and Emergency department of Holby City Hospital. A long-running UK drama series depicting the working, and also the private, lives of the hospital staffers, patients, and others attending the small but frantically busy Accident and Emergency department of the large Holby General Hospital.
