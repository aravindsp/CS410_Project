 Gothic Assassins Redux (2016)Summaries From the ashes of a fallen society, a new cluster of assassins will rise, to avenge their dead comrades. After resurrecting her own death, Dalhiax Dax travels backs in time to her adolescence, showing how she became a ruthless assassins at the age of 18, having multiple targets across the globe, trained and handled by a secret society with strong ties to one of the most powerful countries in the world. Still seeking her own identity, soaked in constant bouts of melancholy, the child shows off her unique skills as an Assassin, fearing no one, nowhere. The killing spree evolves until she meets once again with death. Her own nemesis that always roamed her clouded mind. A somber personage, that will leave her at the brink of a new fatidical ending.
