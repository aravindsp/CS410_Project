 Real Stories (2006)Summaries A ground breaking program that is confronting, heart-warming and inspiring. It's the fifth best current affairs program on Australian television. Journalist Jennifer Adams presents a new comedic show poking fun at Australian current affairs programs. Created by comedians Hamish Blake, Andy Lee, Ryan Shelton and Tim Bartley, Real Stories is, as its creators call it, the fifth best current affairs show on television.
