 Tales from Beyond (2004)Summaries A young couple looking for a gift enter a mysterious bookstore where they are magically transported into the worlds of the books. When a trendy young couple enter a quaint-looking antiquarian bookstore seeking a present for their friend, they find more than they bargained for. A mysterious shopkeeper takes them into the world of his books, leading them through four amazing stories. What happens next they could never have imagined!
