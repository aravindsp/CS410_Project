 Bala Nagamma (1942)Summaries When a young man discovers that his mother was kidnapped by a wicked wizard, he decides to save her. Bala Nagamma is a 1942 Indian Tamil film, directed by Vedantham Ragahvaiah and produced by DN Raju, SN Raju and P Venkatapathi Raju. The film stars N.T. Rama Rao, SVR, and Anjali Devi in lead roles.
