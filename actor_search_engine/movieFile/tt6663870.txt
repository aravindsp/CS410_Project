 Michael Jackson Feat. Janet Jackson: Scream (1995 Video)Summaries Michael Jackson and Janet Jackson star in this short-film to spread awareness on the media's constant slander on various artists. Michael Jackson and Janet Jackson perform in the music video "Scream " from the album "HIStory: Past, Present and Future, Book I" recorded for Epic Records. The black and white video features Michael Jackson and his sister Janet Jackson as they sing and dance in a futuristic setting. Shatterdaymorn In the video, a sign appears with the logo of the song's title. The scene then changes a spacecraft traveling past Earth before the camera zooms in inside. Then, the lights came on and reveal Michael and Janet inside a small hole. Suddenly, the spacecraft zooms out through the outer part of the galaxy, sending them screaming. In the first verse, Michael is seen in the room where he is floating on gravity. Then, Michael is shown in a room with thousands of guitars where he breaks one while Janet sings the next verse. Then, it shows Michael dancing on the wall while Janet looks at her nail before playing a game of tag. Then, it shows Michael and Janet in a room where they are using a remote control by changing pictures of Gregory Peck, art painting, statues, etc. Next, it shows both Michael and Janet in the media room where they are playing a video game which Michael wins.
