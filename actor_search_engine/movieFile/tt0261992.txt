 Winter Break (2003)Summaries Four post-college men living in Aspen try to figure out what to do with their lives. Viscerally experience a "year off" in Aspen, Colorado with a group of recent college grads who have elected to defer graduate school and career opportunities to ski, snow-board, climb, party, fall in love and basically live life to its absolute fullest out in this spectacular mountain town.
