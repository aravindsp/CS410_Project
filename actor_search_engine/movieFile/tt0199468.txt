 Discworld II: Mortality Bytes! (1996 Video Game)Summaries Rincewind, the wizard, is recovering from the adventure he had in the first game but all too soon he is called in to solve another mystery. Second game based on Terry Pratchett's famous Discworld series. Rincewind, the wizard, is recovering from the adventure he had in the first game but all too soon he is called in to solve another mystery. Death has disappeared and departed souls are not leaving their bodies and are walking about the Discworld as the confused "undead". Rincewind is ordered to find Death but not only is that a major problem in itself, but when he does find him he discovers that Death thinks that nobody wants him. How exactly do you convince a being that can terminate everybody's existence that he needed? - it's Rincewind's and your task to complete...
