 Rock Bottom (I) (2010)Summaries Fired from his job as a SCUBA instructor in Hawaii, Jack Lamarr heads back home to the Midwest and finds himself working as a woman's self defense instructor at the local YWCA. Everything appears to be going well until he becomes infatuated with one of his students. Surrounded by the one thing he wants, Jack slowly becomes the threat he taught the women to protect themselves against, forcing this unrequited romance to land him lower than he ever was before.
