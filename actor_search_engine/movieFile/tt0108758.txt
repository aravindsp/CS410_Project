 Earth 2 (19941995)Summaries Colonists, crash-landed on an alien planet, begin the long trek to their originally designated landing place, facing alien and human threats. In the future, the human race is living in space stations orbiting a dead Earth. Faced with a debilitating disease attacking her young son, Devon Adair (Debrah Farentino) and a band of non-conformists leave their artificial environment and travel to a distant planet, where they begin a new life for themselves.
