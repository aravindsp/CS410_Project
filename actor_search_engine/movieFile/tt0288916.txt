 Bennie (1997 )Summaries A puppet series based on international animal tales. For children from 4 up. The fairy-tale world of Mrs van Santen comes to life as soon as six-year-old Benny steps into her house. In each episode she tells an exciting story illustrated by strange and everyday objects in her house. The stories have their origins in different cultures.
