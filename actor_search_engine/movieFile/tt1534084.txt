 In Gold We Trust (2010)Summaries A group of adventurers make off with 600 kilograms of gold in French Guiana, but when their helicopter crashes in the jungle, gold fever takes over. A group of adventurers sets out to rob a gold mine in the heart of French Guiana. But the operation does not go as planned, and, during their flight, their helicopter must land in disaster in the middle of the jungle. They got their hands on six hundred kilograms of gold - but now have to carry it on their backs in a very hostile environment. The booty becomes a burden. The seven fugitives, four men, two women, plunge into the jungle. The climate, insects, fatigue, the threat of pursuers - Everything contributes to making their long march impossible.
