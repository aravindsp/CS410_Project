 El fantasma del Gran Hotel (2009 )Summaries Amrico Esquivel, the owner of the Gran Hotel, is tortured to death by his own employees, who want to seize the treasure that the old man hides. Irene Buenaventura goes to work as an elevator operator at the old hotel, but her true mission is to discover the culprits of Amrico's death using her ability to communicate with the dead. There she falls in love with Miguel Toro, hired by the assassins to find the treasure, without knowing the truth about Don Amrico. Love between them will only be possible when they manage to overcome the stalking of the living and the secrets kept by the dead who inhabit the Grand Hotel.
