 Later Today (19992000)Summaries Program features a mixture of news, entertainment and issue-related segments, covering everything from medical advice, parenting, and fashion to health, financial matters, cooking and fitness. Dedicated to the news and entertainment industry, Later Today is an hour extension of the Today Show, and features three very talented women, who host the show from 30 Rockefeller Plaza in New York.
