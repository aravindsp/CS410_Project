 Maximum Risk (1996)Summaries While seeking answers regarding the death of the twin brother he never knew he had, a French cop and his sibling's fiance find themselves pursued by corrupt FBI agents and the Russian mafia. Alain Moreau didn't know he had a brother, even a twin brother - until he found him laying dead on the street. To reveal the truth about his twin, he now has to continue in his footsteps - he simply has to become Mikhail, travel across the Atlantic and sink into the world that ended his brothers days. Here he meets his brothers beautiful girlfriend Alex, who doesn't suspect a thing for starters. She works as a waitress in a pub, owned by the Russian mafia, and when they find out Alain's true intentions, all hell breaks out...
