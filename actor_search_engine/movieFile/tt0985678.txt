Eliseo, a photographer in Mexico City, becomes obsessed with his neighbour, Maria. When Maria is murdered by a Spanish nationalist, Eliseo's obsession turns him into the prime suspect. Now, he must find a way to prove his innocence while avoiding being captured by the police or murdered by the same killer who has killed several Vasc separatists.
