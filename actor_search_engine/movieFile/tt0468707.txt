 Day Shift (2005)Summaries Reality shifts in this short comedy where the roles between whites and Latinos are inverted. Julio Perez is just trying to have a normal day-unfortunately, he doesn't live in a normal world. Now that the Latinos are in charge, police look like gang members, illegal Canadians sell fruit by the street corners and getting to work has become a huge pain the a**...
