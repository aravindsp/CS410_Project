 La septima puerta (2004 )Summaries Jenny Candela and Toms Cabal Amado are two journalists who are looking for news of paranormal and terrifying events. Whenever something abnormal was presented they would go to the scene to investigate and solve cases that are not very eventual in everyday life such as ghost appearances, strange noises, strange movements, and many more supernatural things. Everything becomes so difficult that they need someone else, and that's when Luisa Zapata arrives and is hired, since then abnormalities begin to appear in medical hospitals, mental hospitals and asylums, in old houses, caves and lonely places, to find facts to be solved.
