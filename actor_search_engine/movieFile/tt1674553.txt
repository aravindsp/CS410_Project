 Penoza (20102017)Summaries After her husband is assassinated, housewife Carmen reluctantly has to take charge of the criminal business of her family. Penoza takes us to the Amsterdam underworld where we meet drug lord Frans and wife Carmx. After their son, at 10, threatens a child with a gun, Carmen presents Frans with an ultimatum: get out of the criminal life or lose your family. But they end up losing Frans, who's shot dead, leaving Carmen to deal with the debts he leaves behind, not to mention other unfinished business.
