 The Body Stealers (1969)Summaries In Britain, bodies of NATO paratroopers are being snatched during routine jumps by a mysterious red-beam of alien origin. A British military paratrooper disappears in mid-air during a jump from an army plane. Two investigators, Patrick Allen and Neil Connery, try to unravel how this happened.
