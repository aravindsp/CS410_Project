 Utopa (2003)Summaries The orphan Adrin Vega has the ability of foreseeing the future and is adopted by the bookseller Samuel. He joins Samuel's group of clairvoyants called Utopia that protects people that will make a difference and Samuel teaches him how to control his power. Years later, Adrin fails in stopping a deranged Utopia member from exploding a car bomb and the wife and daughter of the police inspector Herv are murdered and Herv is blinded by the glass window. Adrin decides to leave the group, but Samuel convinces him to participate in his last assignment, saving the life of the school teacher ngela that has joined an evil sect. Meanwhile Herv is hired by ngela's mother to brainwash her daughter and bring her back home. Claudio Carvalho, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil It looks like we don't have a for this title yet.
