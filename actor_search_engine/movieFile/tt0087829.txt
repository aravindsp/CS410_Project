 Odd Jobs (1986)Summaries Five college buddies start their own moving business only to find themselves tangling with sleazy competitors and the mob. Four students who work on different jobs during their vacations in summer are tired of doing the same thing every year. Therefore they decide to open up a moving company together and confront themselves with the former boss of one of them. Volker Boehm When five fraternity buddies leave school for their summer jobs, Max ( Paul Reiser ) starts work with The Cabrizzi Brothers moving company: Dwight ( Robert Townsend and Byron ( Paul Provenza ) start caddying at a golf course: while Woody ( Scott McGinnis ) waits tables and Roy ( Rick Overton ) attempts to sell nuclear-powered vacuums. When none of their jobs works out, the guys get together and decide to form their own company. Max recommends the moving business. There's only one problem, but it's a big one - The Cabrizzi Brothers. The Cabrizzis are about to demonstrate that brotherly love doesn't extend to strangers on their turf, and they're set to flatten the frat boys, unless they boys use that college education to work their way out of trouble.
