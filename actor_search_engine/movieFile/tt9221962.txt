 Aumenta que Rock 'n RollSummaries The movie follows the story of Rdio Fluminense, the first rock n'roll FM radio in Brazil in the early 1980's, also known as "a Maldita". Created by Luis Antonio Mello, the radio made history and conquered many viewers ever since its inception in 1982, broadcasting the rock n'roll of its era.
