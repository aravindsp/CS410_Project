 The Assault (1998)Summaries A female cop assigned to protect a dead drug dealer's girlfriend from mob assassins takes her to a home for battered wives. When revenge is the only option, the emotionally scarred women are forced to fight back. Ace police detective Stacy Palermo ( Stacie Randall ) thinks that babysitting a homicide witness is going to be a boring assignment. When the murderer turns out to be Blade, a vicious and powerful drug lord, Stacy and her witness must flee to a remote halfway house for abused women with Blade on their trail. In the tradition of "Die Hard," "The Assault" is non-stop action and edge-of-your-seat suspense.
