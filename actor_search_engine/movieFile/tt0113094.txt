 Cyberella: Forbidden Passions (1996)Summaries Your wildest fantasies are possible in this look at afterlife encounters. This cyber-seductress uses her special skills to fulfill your most intimate dreams. Mara's soul is left in her computer when she dies while using a virtual reality program. She is given four chances to learn the "lesson" of life so she can move on to heaven. Each lesson involves a member of the opposite, or same, sex who is using another virtual reality program.
