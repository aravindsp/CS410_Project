 V.I.P. (19982002)Summaries A team of 5, 3 professional bodyguards, a receptionist and the ex hot dog vendor and figurehead, Vallery/ Pamela Anderson , make up Vallery Irons Protection/VIP, bodyguards for celebrities. Campy syndicated series about Vallery Irons, a girl working at a hotdog stand who accidently saves a celebrity and is mistaken for a bodyguard. She and a team of beautiful bodyguards form a bodyguard agency called V.I.P., which stands for Vallery Irons Protection.
