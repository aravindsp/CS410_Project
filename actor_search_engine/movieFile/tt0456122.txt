 Os Desafinados (2008)Summaries 'Slightly Out Of Tune' is about a group of friends who come of age during the political turmoil that plagued Brazil in the 1960s and '70s. Together, these musicians form a band called "Os Desafinados" and become part of the groundbreaking Brazilian musical movement called Bossa Nova.
