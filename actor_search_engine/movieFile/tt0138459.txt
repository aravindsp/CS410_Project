Shortly after moving to Rittersburg to take over duties as the lone "Schattenjager", Gabriel Knight is called by the townsfolk to investigate the killing of a child believed to be caused by a werewolf. While Gabriel is away on the case, Grace Nakimura leaves New Orleans for Rittersburgh to conduct research and keep an eye on him. Grace soon learns that Gabriel's case goes back centuries and involves King Ludwig II. Meanwhile, Gabriel's investigation leads him to a small private hunting club, where he believes the culprit may be hiding. The two are pulled deeper into the horrific events, leading Grace out to stop the unrelenting nightmare and Gabriel in a fight for his very own soul. Andrew Kasch <akasch@darkvortex.com> After a terrifying experience with Voodoo in New Orleans, Gabriel Knight decides to move to Rittersberg, Germany where he inherits an old castle from a relative he knew for only a short time. He also holds the title of Schattenjager, or "Shadow Hunter," whose duty is to hunt down the supernatural. One night, while writing his next horror novel, Gabriel is visited by villagers who believe a werewolf murdered a farmer's daughter. Meanwhile, Grace Nakimura tries to join Gabriel in Germany, but investigates the werewolf case by herself. Only when the two uncover a lot of clues and finally meet up will they discover an old curse that, once again, will pull Gabriel Knight into darkness.
