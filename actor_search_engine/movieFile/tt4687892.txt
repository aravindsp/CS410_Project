 Good Girls Revolt (20152016)Summaries A look at the personal and professional lives of employees at an American news magazine in the late 1960s. Based on the true story, Good Girls Revolt follows the lives of women working in fictional "News of the Week" newsroom (based on Newsweek magazine) as "researchers" under a group of male reporters.
