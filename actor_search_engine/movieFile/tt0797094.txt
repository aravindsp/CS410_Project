In the middle of the exotic landscapes of the Colombian Caribbean Coast, where the vallenato music has magic effects in their habitants: (calms down the crying of the babies, turns the direction of the storms, increases the egg production of the hens, cheers up the cotton harvesters) lives Poncho, an eleven year old boy, a talented vallenato performer who has two dreams in his life: to be a Vallenato King and to win over the love of his life, Sara Maria. Dreams that wont be easy to achieve because of the economical difficulties he faces and that will make him grow up before time and because of his father a male chauvinist who constantly humiliates him. Besides that Poncho has to suffer the constant attacks of Pepe, the other suitor of Sara Maria who wins over the girl by lies and frauds, and that will not let Poncho achieve one of his goals. Like that in the middle of the magical journey that he will make in search for an accordion, Poncho will meet some people of his land that will teach him the values of friendship, kindness, love and of dreams that will turn him into a magical character known as The angel of the accordion.
