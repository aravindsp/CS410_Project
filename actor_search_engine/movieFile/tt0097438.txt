 Gleaming the Cube (1989)Summaries A young avid skateboarder uncovers a government conspiracy while investigating his adoptive brother's mysterious death. Brian's adopted brother is killed when he discovers that the shop he works in sends weapons to Vietnam instead of medications. To the police it looks like suicide, but Brian knows better so he skates off to investigate the murder himself. Lars J.
