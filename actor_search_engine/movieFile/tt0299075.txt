 Nothing Like Dreaming (2004)Summaries A film about a troubled fire artist and a teenage girl in rural Vermont. A film about a fire artist and a troubled teenage girl in rural Vermont. In their fortuitous encounter, forged by fire, he makes peace with the past and she takes control of her destiny. Together they build a mysterious musical instrument that provides a path to their healing. The story takes place in Vermont, against the backdrop of the state capitol, where civil union laws are being debated along with gun control and DWI's.
