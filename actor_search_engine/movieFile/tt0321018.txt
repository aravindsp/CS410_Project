 What I Like About You (20022006)Summaries When Holly's father is transferred to Japan, she is sent to live with her big sister Valerie in New York City, turning Valerie's life upside down. When Holly ( Amanda Bynes ) is 16, her father takes a job in Japan and she moves in with her strait-laced sister Valerie ( Jennie Garth ) in New York City. Val finally has both a great career and a great boyfriend in a carefully organized life on the Upper West Side when Holly moves in and tries to become a part of her sister's fabulous life.
