 Top Boy (2011 )Summaries Two London drug dealers ply their lucrative trade at a public housing estate in East London. A thriving but underground drugs business is being run by Dushane ( Ashley Walters ) and his friend Sully ( Kano ) to become the richest men on the block. To live rich Dushane and Sully go into partnership with top don Bobby Raikes ('Geoff Bell (I)'). Rivalry with Kamale ( Scorcher ) and his gang results in their loan from Raikes being stolen, and they swear revenge. Thirteen-year old Ra'Nell ( Malcolm Kamulete ) gradually gets lured into the gang and drug business as his mother Lisa ( Sharon Duncan-Brewster ) has a breakdown and is hospitalised - with devastating consequences.
