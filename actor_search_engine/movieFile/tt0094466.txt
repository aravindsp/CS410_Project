 Freddy's Nightmares (19881990)Summaries Freddy, the dream serial killer, hosts this anthology of stories set in Springwood. The evil, sinister killer of the "Nightmare On Elm Street" movies, Freddy Krueger, hosts this show, where each week, he shows us a tale of evil and death about the lives of people who live in Springwood.
