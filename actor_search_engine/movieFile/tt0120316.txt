 The Wedding Party (1997)Summaries The romantic myth is exposed for Guy when he is plagued by memories of an old girlfriend on his wedding day. Guy marries Lizzie. Dreamy Guy wanders around at his own wedding reception, recalling fond days of the girlfriend that got away; his long-time, de facto relationship with the warm and funny Jenny. L.H.
