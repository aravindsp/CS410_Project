 Mexico City (2000)Summaries A woman's desperate search for her brother in the capital of Mexico. After her divorce and the death of her two children, Mitch is headed for Oaxaca with her photographer brother Sam. During their 1-day layover in Mexico City, Sam goes out for a night on the town--and doesn't return. Mitch goes to the American embassy for help and starts her own search with help from Pedro, a cabbie. She finds the cantina where Sam was drinking and stands up to a gang leader to learn some of what happened that night. He gives her Sam's camera, and the photos on the last roll of film become the object of a hunt by some very determined criminals. Can Mitch find Sam, learn what he saw that night, and stay safe?
