 Wirey Spindell (1999)Summaries A whimsical look back at the life of a man who is nervous about his upcoming wedding. A semi-autobiographical tale of Eric Schaeffer, who as Wirey Spindell, is seeing a marriage counsler with his future wife because he is nervous about his upcoming wedding.
