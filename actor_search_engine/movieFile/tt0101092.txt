 The Exile (1991 )Summaries John Phillips was an undercover agent for the United States. Then his life was taken away after another agent had a secret to keep and decided to pin Phillips for a crime he didn't commit. Knowing the he cannot go back home because he would be arrested, he stays exiled in France under the name John Stone. There is only two people he can trust: Charlie Cabbott, A U.S. official who worked to get Phillips' name cleared and Danni Montreau, a French cop whom he would help solves cases with.
