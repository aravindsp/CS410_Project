 Jidder (1998)Summaries Tony is a young man on the loose, he's having trouble in school and with his family. Together with his homies Bob and Pedro he does some smaller crimes (like stealing sausages). But one day Tony meets the local crime boss Claes Douglas at the gym and he leads Tony in to a new world of guns and drugs. Claes Douglas suggests that Tony does a hit with Bob and Pedro for him.
