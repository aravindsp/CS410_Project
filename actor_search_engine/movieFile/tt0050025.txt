 Have Gun - Will Travel (19571963)Summaries The adventures of a gentlemanly gunfighter-for-hire. Professional gunfighter Paladin was a West Point graduate who, after the Civil War, settled into San Francisco's Hotel Carlton, where he awaited responses to his business card: over the picture of a chess knight is written "Have Gun, Will Travel...Wire Paladin, San Francisco."
