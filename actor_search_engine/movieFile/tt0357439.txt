 Arriba Michoacn (1987)Summaries Michoacan, is a land of law abiding men and wild women. In the villages of El Sauce El Alto and El Sauce el Bajo, the men have left, and now the women have come to compete for political power. When it comes to settling differences between these modern amazons, methods vary from an all out brawl, visiting the justice of peace, another brawl, music, dancing, and love.
