 The Investigator (2010 Video Game)Summaries A first-of-its-kind 3D photorealistic online interactive game of skill incorporating live-action content that puts the viewer in the middle of the action to solve a murder mystery. An online interactive murder mystery game. To experience The Investigator, gamers must pro-actively solve a crime and assume the role of the "hero" homicide investigator; during the course of solving an original storyline and move up in rank.
