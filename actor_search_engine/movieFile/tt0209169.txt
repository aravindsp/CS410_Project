 The Mysterious Moon-Men of Canada (1988)Summaries A filmmaker searches for two Canadians who flew to the moon in 1959, but because they were Canadian, were too modest to tell anybody about it. Hired to make an industrial movie about a blind cyclist riding across Canada to raise money for glaucoma research, disgruntled filmmaker Brownie McFadden stumbles across an extraordinary letter: it describes a trip taken to the moon in 1959, a full ten years before Apollo.
