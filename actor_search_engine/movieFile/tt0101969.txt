 Grand Canyon (1991)Summaries The fates of several people are intertwining randomly. Their sympathy of each other faces multiple differences in their lifestyles. Grand Canyon revolved around six residents from different backgrounds whose lives intertwine in modern-day Los Angeles. At the center of the film is the unlikely friendship of two men from different races and classes brought together when one finds himself in jeopardy in the other's rough neighborhood.
