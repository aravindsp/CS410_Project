 Desired (2011)Summaries With a curb-crawling serial killer targeting prostitutes, Joshua scours Auckland's underbelly to find the woman who keeps running away, before it's too late. In the aftermath of a tragic event, Desired charts the breakdown of a marriage between a detective and an ex-prostitute. Unable to put the past behind them, Joshua (Cameron) throws himself into his police work, while Sarah (Tabron) seeks to numb the pain through drugs and sexual encounters. No longer able to connect with one another, they are overwhelmed by intense memories that shed light on the history of their relationship.
