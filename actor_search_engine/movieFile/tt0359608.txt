 A Little Crazy (2003)Summaries "A Little Crazy" is a family drama that centers on Theo, a man in his early thirties, who on the day he decides to quit his dead-end job receives news of his father's suicide attempt and learns his wife may be pregnant. Theo must return home to help take care of his father and finds himself surrounded by his eccentric family. "A Little Crazy" is one family's quest for sanity.
