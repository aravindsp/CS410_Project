Itching to leave small-town life in Shelbyville, Tennessee, five friends are sent into a frenzy when they learn hometown-girl- turned-Hollywood-star Sondra Locke may be returning. Believing she's their ticket out, they do everything they can to make it happen. 2005 winner of the Bluegrass Independent Film Festival Grand Prize for Best Feature Film and the Rome International Film Festival's Special Jury Prize, Our Very Own is a heartwarming, hope-filled story about the power of friendship, family and believing in yourself. As Shelbyville buzzes with excitement with the impeding return of famous actress Sondra Locke , five teenagers face decisions that will affect their futures. Melora has dreams of following Locke's path to stardom, while Clancy is preoccupied by his parents' impending bankruptcy.
