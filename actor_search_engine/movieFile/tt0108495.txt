 Bad Blood (1994)Summaries A trucker (Lamas) has to come home to help fight mobsters after his brother who embezzled $5,000,000 from them. Travis Blackstone, former cop was sent to prison because he destroyed the proofs for his brother's guilt. Now, he is again free but Rhonda, his former love and the present lover of his brother Franklin informs him that Franklin is again in trouble owing $5 million. Travis leaves her to help his brother.
