 This Is Your Life (19552003)Summaries The UK version of the popular US show. Eamonn Andrews (later Michael Aspel ) surprises celebrities by presenting them with the Big Red Book before taking them into the studio to tell viewers the story of their life, featuring guest appearances by members of their family, friends and colleagues.
