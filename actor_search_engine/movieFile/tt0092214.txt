 Wildcats (1986)Summaries A rookie high school football coach has a harder time than she expected trying to whip her tough inner city team into shape. Molly is a high school track coach who knows just as much about football as anyone else on the planet. When the football coach's position becomes vacant, she applies for the job, despite expecting sniggers from fellow staff members and her former husband.
