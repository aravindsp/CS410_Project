 Dynasty (19811989)Summaries The trials and tribulations of two feuding families, the Carringtons and the Colbys, both oil rich families in Denver, Colorado. The saga of a wealthy Denver family in the oil business: Blake Carrington, the patriarch; Krystle, his former secretary and wife; his children: Adam, lost in childhood after a kidnapping; Fallon, pampered and spoiled; Steven, openly gay; and Amanda, hidden from him by his ex-wife, the conniving Alexis.
