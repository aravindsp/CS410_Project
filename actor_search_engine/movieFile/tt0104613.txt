 Killer Image (1992)Summaries After his brother's murder, Max Oliver (John Pyper-Ferguson) suspects the last photographs his brother took provide the key to a sprawling political cover-up. When Max Oliver learns his photographer brother has been killed, he suspects it was no random murder. And when he finds his brothers' last photos of a powerful senator and a prostitute, Max gets a clear picture of a deadly political cover-up. Seeking to expose his brother's killer, Max enters a murderous game of cat and mouse, stalked by a cold-blooded assassin who has Max dead in his sights.
