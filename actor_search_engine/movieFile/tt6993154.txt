 The AbodeSummaries In the 1700s, a young, handsome native American named Talako is caught with the wife of a merciless pirate known as Redbeard, who then orders him to be burned alive at the stake. Learning of Talako's execution, Redbeard's wife Lara hangs herself. Talako's spirit never ascends and haunts anyone who comes near the old house.
