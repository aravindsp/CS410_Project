 East of Hollywood (2015)Summaries A mockumentary about an Asian American actor who attempts to be more Asian in hopes of landing a recurring role on a Network TV show. "East of Hollywood" is a short film that deals with the trials and tribulations of the average Asian American actor. Shot as a documentary, this comedy exaggerates the, sometimes degrading, nature of the casting process. Filled with cultural references, memorable characters, and a poignant story, this film raises important questions about Asian representation in the media, while still making audiences laugh out loud.
