 Wooden Woman (20042005)Summaries Marissa is a sweet, caring woman who loses her father in a wild forest fire, then becomes the owner of her father's ranch, "Las Cspides". She is deeply in love with Cesar, but their wedding is interrupted by a woman who claims to be pregnant by him.
