 What Would You Do? (2009 )Summaries A social experiment show which takes controversial situations to the streets of America to see how everyday citizens will react. When you think no one is watching... what would you do? Using hidden cameras, What Would You Do? establishes everyday scenarios and then captures people's reactions. Whether people are compelled to act or mind their own business, John Quiones reports on their split-second and often surprising decision-making process.
