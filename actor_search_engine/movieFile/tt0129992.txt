 High Voltage (1997)Summaries When Johnny Clay (Antonio Sabato Jr.) and his gal pal Jane Logan (Shannon Lee, daughter of martial arts legend Bruce Lee) pull off a bank heist, they accidentally uncover a money laundering front created by the local Mafia. A bank, where Bruce Lee's daughter plays manager, is a Vietnamese mob front. When a group aborts their try,mobsters try to kill them. Much gun play,but robbers and bank manager, who has turned on the mob,show off martial arts skills. A preemptive strike kills some, but fails to kill the boss. A group of young adults robbing a bank accidentally discover a money-laundering scheme by the Vietnamese mob, prompting mobsters to come after them.
