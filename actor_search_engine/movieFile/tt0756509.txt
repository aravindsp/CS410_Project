 Kyle XY (20062009)Summaries A family takes in a formerly institutionalized teen savant who is missing standard human behaviors such as anger, joy, and love. A Seattle family takes in an amnesiac teen (Dallas), whom they eventually learn has mysterious powers and psychic abilities, the result of a secret human cloning experiment conducted years earlier. The family helps teach Kyle human behaviors such as anger, joy and love, and guards him against forces who seek to use Kyle for their own nefarious purposes.
