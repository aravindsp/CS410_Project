 Why Women Kill (2019 )Summaries An anthology series that follows three women in different decades all living in the same house, as they deal with infidelity and betrayals in their marriages. Why Women Kill details the lives of three women living in three different decades: a housewife in the '60s, a socialite in the '80s, and a lawyer in 2019, each dealing with infidelity in their marriages.
