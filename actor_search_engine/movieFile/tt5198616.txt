 Eva Dragon (1959)Summaries Her crackling lash stuck fear in the hearts men, until one man stood his ground and won her over. A heartless and fiery Eva Dragon (played by MerleTuazon) turned an entire hacienda in terror, instilling fear in the hearts of its populace. With gun and whiplash, she makes her ruthlessness felt far and wide until she meets her match in Amador Martinez (Fernando Poe Jr.) one of her tenants, in whose hands Eva becomes a woman again.
