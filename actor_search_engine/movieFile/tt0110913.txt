 Pumpkinhead II: Blood Wings (1993)Summaries Thrill-seeking teenagers resurrect a demon from his grave and a bloody rampage for revenge begins. After a group of teenagers indirectly cause an old witch to be burned, they accidently revive Pumpkinhead. This time Pumpkinhead is inhabited by the soul of a deformed orphan killed 30 years before. He goes on a bloody rampage after his tormentors and the teenagers. Meanwhile, a local sheriff tries to solve the mystery and stop the murders.
