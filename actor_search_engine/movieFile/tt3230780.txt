 The Returned (2015)Summaries A small town's residents are stunned when recently deceased locals begin returning from the dead. What should be a miracle soon becomes a nightmare. Focuses on a small town that is turned upside down when several local people, who have been presumed dead suddenly reappear, having not aged and unaware of their own fatality. As they attempt to resume their lives, strange phenomena and gruesome murders begin to occur.
