 Corrina, Corrina (1994)Summaries In 1959, a widower hires a kindly housekeeper/nanny to care for his seven-year-old daughter. When Manny Singer's wife dies, his young daughter Molly becomes mute and withdrawn. To help cope with looking after Molly, he hires sassy housekeeper Corrina Washington, who coaxes Molly out of her shell and shows father and daughter a whole new way of life.
