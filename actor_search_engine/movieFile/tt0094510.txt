 Midnight Caller (19881991)Summaries An ex-cop hosts a late-night talk radio program while getting involved with his listeners' problems in his off-time. Jack Killian is an ex-cop in San Francisco. He quit the police force after accidentally shooting his partner. He was approached by Devon King, the manager of a local radio station, and accepted a job as a talk-back host. Ever the humanitarian, Jack (together with his side-kick Billy Po) often gets personally involved with the plight of those who call his show.
