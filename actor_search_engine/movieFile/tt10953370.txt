 The Genius of Gianni Versace (2020)Summaries Feature-length documentary film celebrating the extraordinary life and career of influential Italian fashion designer Gianni Versace, beginning with his birth in the city of Reggio Calabria, Italy on 2 December 1946, and growing up with his elder brother Santo Versace and younger sister Donatella Versace, along with their father and dressmaker mother, Francesca, through building his financial empire, and showcasing his many extraordinary homes including his Miami Beach Palazzo, the Casa Casuarina.
