 The Good Girl (2002)Summaries A discount store clerk strikes up an affair with a stock boy who considers himself the incarnation of Holden Caulfield. The plot revolves around a young married woman whose mundane life takes a turn for the worse when she strikes up a passionate and illicit affair with an oddball discount-store stock boy who thinks he's Holden Caulfield.
