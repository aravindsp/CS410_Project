 Flick (I) (2000)Summaries Simon Thomas blindly enters the world of independent film making while trying to juggle his social life, a job, a new relationship, and an ex-girlfriend that just won't give up. Not to mention, he hasn't even started filming yet. Bill Tedford <wtedford@hotmail.com> Simon, a young film-buff, fights back in boredom & frustration with the film industry by writing and directing his own movie. Having never even attempted to construct a feature length presentation or any form of movie, Simon, with the help of his friends, experience the hardships of creating an independent film.
