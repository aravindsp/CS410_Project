 Nostradamus (I) (2000)Summaries A policeman must stop a medieval cult, who plan to bring about armageddon by summoning the spirit of Nostradamus. Rob Estes plays a homicide detective investigating a series of strange multiple murders, assisted by an oddball FBI agent, played by Joely Fisher, who is also clairvoyant. The apparent perpetrator is a time travelling assassin, come all the way from the 15th century seeking to bring about Armageddon.
