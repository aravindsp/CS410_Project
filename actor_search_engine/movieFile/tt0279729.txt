 Chenchu Lakshmi (I) (1958)Summaries Vishnu's wife Mahalakshmi's parents are reborn as tribal people in a tribe called Chenchu as they are cursed by a sage. There they give birth to a beautiful girl named Chenchitha. Vishu after killing Hiranyakasipu wanders in that forest and sees Chenchitha and changes himself as a handsome young man Narahari.
