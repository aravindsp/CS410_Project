 Contre nature (2008)Summaries On an isolated island in the Atlantic, a group of villagers have created a very tightly knit community. Igor, a teenager on the island, feels like a prisonner of the island and of the forbidden relationship he has with his cousin Claire. This relationship forces her family to flee the pressure of the island, which unleashes hidden demons which everyone thought were buried.
