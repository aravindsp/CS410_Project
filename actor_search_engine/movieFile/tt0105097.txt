 Parenti serpenti (1992)Summaries During the Christmas celebrations, a surprising announcement takes a family by surprise and threatens to break the delicate balance that regulates the familial relationships. Like every year, everyone is going back to the ancestral family home for the Christmas holidays. But this time, the old folks have a good surprise for them: they have decided to go live with one of their children and bequeath the family house to him/her.
