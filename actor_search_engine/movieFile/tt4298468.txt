 DELKA: Stand-Up Tall or Fall (2015)Summaries A young gymnast immigrates from the Iron Curtain of Bulgaria finding liberation to the Land of the Free through an International psychedelic Traveling Circus. Inspired by true events; An immigration story of a gymnast from Bulgaria with a heart of gold and stars in her eyes. She is adopted into a traveling Circus of psychedelic characters after being raped and finds herself on a journey, to the Land of the Free* Will she live her Dream?
