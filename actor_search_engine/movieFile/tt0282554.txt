 Hello Brother (1994)Summaries A quiet and mature man meets with his twin - who is exactly his opposite. After giving birth to twin sons, Deva and Raja, Mrs. Varma is separated from Raja, goes into shock and gets confined to a wheelchair abroad. Years later, in order to get a matured and sober Deva married, she, Deva, and her husband return home.
