 The Willies (1990)Summaries A harmless backyard camp out becomes an unforgettable night of chills and thrills for three young boys as they share their favorite scary stories. Two brothers camping with their cousin try to frighten each other by telling stories. There are two main narrations: one involves strange happenings at an elementary school; the other, a teenage boy with a peculiar interest.
